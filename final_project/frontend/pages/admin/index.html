📤 SMTP Configuration

📖 How to Activate Your SMTP

Choose your email provider and follow the steps to get your SMTP credentials.

📨 Gmail  smtp.gmail.com ▼ Show steps
Step 1: Go to your Google Account → Security
Step 2: Turn on 2-Step Verification if not already on
Step 3: Search for "App passwords" in the Security page
Step 4: Select app: Mail — Select device: Other → type ExamPro
Step 5: Click Generate and copy the 16-character password shown
Step 6: Fill the form above:
  • Host: smtp.gmail.com   Port: 587
  • Username: your Gmail address  • Password: the 16-character app password
⚠️ Do NOT use your normal Gmail login password — App Password only.
💼 Outlook / Hotmail  smtp.office365.com ▼ Show steps
Step 1: Log in at outlook.live.com → Settings → View all Outlook settings
Step 2: Go to Mail → Sync email and enable SMTP access
Step 3: Fill the form above:
  • Host: smtp.office365.com   Port: 587
  • Username: your full Outlook email  • Password: your Outlook password
  • SSL/TLS: leave unchecked (STARTTLS handles it on port 587)
🌐 Zoho Mail  smtp.zoho.com ▼ Show steps
Step 1: Log in at mail.zoho.com → Settings → Mail Accounts → SMTP → Enable it
Step 2: Fill the form above:
  • Host: smtp.zoho.com   Port: 587
  • Username: your Zoho email  • Password: Zoho password (or App Password if 2FA is on)
🖥️ cPanel / Hosting Email  mail.yourdomain.com ▼ Show steps
Step 1: Log into cPanel → Email Accounts → create or select an email (e.g. [email protected])
Step 2: Click Connect Devices on that account to see SMTP details
Step 3: Fill the form above:
  • Host: mail.yourdomain.com   Port: 587 or 465
  • Username: the full email address  • Password: that email's password
